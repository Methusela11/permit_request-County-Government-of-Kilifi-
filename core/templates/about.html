Permit Request

The Permit Request System is a digital initiative developed by the Department of Environment, Forestry, Natural Resources & Solid Waste Management under the County Government of Kilifi. Our mission is to promote environmental responsibility by streamlining the process of applying for and issuing environmental permits online, across Kilifi County.
